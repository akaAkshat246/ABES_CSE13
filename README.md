MoodScape is a responsive emotional workspace that dynamically transforms its theme, motion, assistant guidance, analytics, and focus tools around the user's current psychological state. It bridges productivity and mindfulness by aligning active digital environments with real-time cognitive capacity.
**Designed & Crafted by:** Akshat Vats (ABES Engineering College, Ghaziabad)

---

## Key Features

### 1.Smart Mood Scanning & HSL Mapping
* **Sentiment Analysis:** Type a brief journal entry (e.g., *"I feel slightly stressed but focused..."*) or choose quick-select presets to instantly adapt the atmosphere.
* **Dynamic HSL Theming:** Automatically shifts the entire site's color palette, virtual environment, and UI motion to match your exact vibe.
* **Intelligent AI Guide:** Syncs real-time assistant feedback to provide appropriate coping strategies or motivation based on your mood.

### 2.Pressure-Free Reflective Planning
* **Cognitive Capacity Sorting:** Organize your daily targets and next steps without rigid deadlines or stress-inducing schedules.
* **Mindful Checklists:** Beautiful, fluid interactive completion markers that promote intentional work.

### 3.Deep Hourglass Focus Timer
* **Fluid CSS Sand Flow:** A visually striking custom-built CSS hourglass physical sand animation to ground your focus session.
* **Flexible Tracking:** Features precise timer reset and buffer-minute keys to easily match your natural workflow rhythm.
* **Acoustic Sanctuary:** Built-in ambient Spotify playlist integration for ideal soundscapes.

### 4.Cozy Cinema Escape
* **Curated Content:** Over 600+ hand-sorted, premium movie recommendations designed to help you wind down post-work.
* **Instant Filtering:** Direct title search, platform badges, and active state filtering to match films flawlessly to your mood.

---

## Tech Stack

* **Frontend Framework:** React.js
* **Styling & Animation:** Custom CSS / Tailwind CSS (featuring fluid physical sand flow transitions)
* **Color Engine:** Dynamic HSL Mapping
* **Media Integration:** Spotify API, Curated Entertainment Database

---

## How It Works

1. **Scan:** Log your state via text input or manual mood selectors (**Happy, Calm, Focused, Stressed, Angry**).
2. **Reflect:** List immediate action items adjusted to your current energy baseline.
3. **Focus:** Start the 25:00 hourglass countdown for distraction-free execution.
4. **Unwind:** Use the curated mood picks to discover high-definition film reviews and recommendations.
